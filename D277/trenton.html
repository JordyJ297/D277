Category Description Fun Fact
Location Capital of New Jersey Trenton is one of the oldest state capitals in the United States.
Founded Founded in 1719 Trenton was originally settled by Quakers in the early 18th century.
Government Trenton City Council The city's government operates under a Mayor-Council form of government.
Population Approximately 84,913 Trenton's population has diverse ethnic and cultural backgrounds.
Land Area 7.66 square miles The city is located along the Delaware River and has a compact size.
Landmarks State House, Old Barracks Museum The New Jersey State House is one of the oldest functioning statehouses in the U.S.
